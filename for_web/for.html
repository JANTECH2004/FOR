About FOR

Bridging the Skills Gap

FOR was founded with a clear mission: to address the growing disconnect between academic education and industry requirements. We identified that many graduates, despite having degrees, lack the practical skills employers seek.

Our comprehensive training programs are designed to transform students into industry-ready professionals through targeted skill development in aptitude, soft skills, verbal communication, and advanced programming concepts like Data Structures and Algorithms.
